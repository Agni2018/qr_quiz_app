# QR Quiz Platform - Features Overview

## Admin Dashboard (Users Dashboard)
- **Analytics**: Displays platform-wide statistics, user engagement metrics, and overall performance summaries.
  - **Certifications**: Allows administrators to authorize and issue digital certificates to users upon successful topic completion.
  - **Messaging Tools**: Features direct messaging capabilities allowing admins to send personalized communications to users directly from their profiles.
- **Manage Topics**: Allows administrators to create, edit, organize, and delete quiz categories and topics.
- **Question Bank**: Provides a centralized repository for admins to add, edit, and manage individual quiz questions.
- **Badges & Challenges**: Enables the creation, tracking, and management of gamification elements like achievement badges and active challenges.
- **Uploaded Files**: Allows administrators to upload, manage, view, and organize study materials and resources for the platform.

## Student Dashboard
- **Progress / Home**: Shows the student's personal learning journey, recent quiz scores, and overall performance analytics.
- **Explore**: Allows students to browse available topics, discover new quizzes, and find subjects to learn.
- **Challenges**: Displays active and past challenges the student can participate in to test their skills and earn rewards.
- **Badges**: Showcases the achievement badges and gamified rewards the student has unlocked or is actively working towards.
- **Leaderboard**: Displays user rankings, comparing the student's performance and scores with peers on the platform.
- **Materials**: Provides access to study resources, uploaded files, and reference documents to help prepare for quizzes.
- **Certificates**: Stores and displays digital certificates the student has earned by successfully completing specific courses or topics.
- **Notifications**: Alerts the student about platform updates, newly unlocked badges, incoming challenges, and other important events or message from admin.
- **Referral**: Allows students to invite friends to the platform using unique referral links or codes to unlock bonuses.
- **Referral Leads**: Tracks the status and onboarding progress of the student's referred friends and their associated rewards.
